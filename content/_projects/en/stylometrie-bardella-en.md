## Objective

Analyze style differences between transcribed oral speeches and written texts by Jordan Bardella, given recurring doubts about whether he authored the latest books published under his name.

## Current state & Next steps

- A first end-to-end pipeline was built, but contradictory results require a revised methodology.
- Compare with additional transcription-vs-written corpora. Corpus selection and new pipeline are in progress.
